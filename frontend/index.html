About constraints
How this generator works
  • Greedy scheduling (FFD): departments are sorted by student count (descending), then each exam is placed into the first shift that fits.
  • Capacity constraint: a shift has Total seats per shift. Each scheduled exam consumes seats equal to its student count.
  • Gap constraint: a department’s exams can’t be within Gap days of each other (same rule as your C++: \(|day - usedDay| \le gap\) is conflict).
  • Rooms + roll numbers: each exam is split into rooms of size Room capacity and assigned roll ranges like CS-001 to CS-050.

Tip: If the calendar is too short, the generator automatically adds more days until everything is placed.
